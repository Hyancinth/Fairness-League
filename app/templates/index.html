📖 How to Play

🎮 Game Rules

  • 🔢 Play 11 rounds of Rock Paper Scissors against R.O.B.
  • 📷 Show your hand gesture to the webcam, then click Play Round.
  • ⏱️ A 3-2-1 countdown will play — hold your gesture steady!
  • 🤝 Ties don't count as a round — you'll replay until there's a winner.
  • 🏆 The player with the most wins after 11 rounds takes the game!

✋ Hand Gestures

  • ✊ Rock — Make a fist (all fingers closed)
  • ✋ Paper — Open hand (all fingers extended)
  • ✌️ Scissors — Extend index + middle finger
💡 Tip: Only show one hand to the camera. For scissors, tilt your hand sideways instead of pointing head-on toward the camera — this helps the recognition work much better!

🤖 About R.O.B.

  • 🧠 R.O.B. has a secret strategy — can you figure it out?
  • 😊 Watch R.O.B.'s emotions change based on the score!
